Soko Plan

Website Planning Document

1. Site Name & Identity

Soko (E-commerce Market)

Why this name? "Soko" means "market" in Swahili. It represents a vibrant, accessible, and community-focused space. Choosing this name reflects a bridge between affordable global manufacturing and local consumers who are looking for high-quality, budget-friendly trending imports.

Proposed Domain: soko-market.shop (or soko-imports.com)

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of Soko is to provide an interactive, lightning-fast, and responsive catalog showcasing trending imported products from China. It serves as a modern prototype proving that affordable consumer goods can be navigated easily through rich client-side features like real-time search, category filtering, and an instant-update shopping cart.

3. Target Audience & Scenarios

Our target audience consists of budget-conscious young professionals, students, and local retail entrepreneurs looking for hot, trending products at competitive prices.

Scenario 1: The Budget-Minded Tech Enthusiast

"I am looking for a trending smart accessory (like wireless earbuds or a smart watch) but my budget is strictly under $25. Can I easily filter out more expensive items on this site to find exactly what fits my wallet?"

Scenario 2: The Returning Shopper

"I've added two different items to my cart, but I want to adjust the quantity of one item and remove the other without the entire web page reloading. Does the cart calculate my new subtotal instantly?"

4. Color Schema

The chosen color palette balances a modern corporate feel with warm, energetic commerce accents. It ensures a high contrast ratio compliant with accessibility (WCAG AA guidelines).

Primary Dark (#0d1b2a) Header, Footer, Dark Backgrounds
Accent Amber (#f4a261) Buttons, Active Links, Highlights
Light Neutral (#e2e8f0) Card Backgrounds, Borders, Alternating Rows
White (#ffffff) Main Content Areas, Text Contrast Zones

5. Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

Soko: Quality Meets Affordability

Used for all page titles (h1), section headings (h2), and sub-headings (h3) to convey a bold, modern, and clean brand voice.

Body Font: Open Sans

The shopping cart calculates totals instantly as items are added or removed.

Used for all standard paragraphs, product descriptions, interface options, and navigational links to guarantee outstanding readability across various screen sizes.
